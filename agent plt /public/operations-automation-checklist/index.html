1. Leads & follow-up

  • New leads wait more than 5 minutes for a first reply
  • Follow-up depends on someone remembering to do it
  • Leads get one reply, then go silent (no 2nd–5th touch)
  • No consistent qualifying questions before a call is booked
  • You discover dropped leads days or weeks later
  • After-hours and weekend inquiries go unanswered

2. Inbox & communication

  • Important emails get buried and answered late
  • The first hour of the day is spent just triaging the inbox
  • The same routine replies get written by hand again and again
  • Messages get missed when the team is heads-down on delivery
  • No clear system for urgent vs. later vs. ignore

3. CRM & records

  • The CRM is always a few days out of date
  • Call notes and next steps get logged inconsistently (or not at all)
  • Deal stages don't reflect what's actually happening
  • Reps spend selling hours on data entry
  • Reporting means manually copying numbers between tools

4. Scheduling & coordination

  • Booking and rescheduling is a back-and-forth email/phone game
  • No-shows happen because reminders are manual or skipped
  • Confirmations and prep info aren't sent automatically
  • Internal handoffs between people drop the ball

5. Documents & admin

  • You chase clients for the same documents repeatedly
  • Invoices, quotes, or proposals are followed up by hand
  • Repetitive document creation eats hours each week
  • Onboarding a new client is a manual checklist someone runs

6. Reporting & visibility

  • You don't get a clear daily picture of what happened
  • Status updates to clients are written manually
  • You only notice problems after they've cost you
  • Key metrics live in your head, not in a report
  • Weekly/monthly reports take hours to assemble
  • No single place shows the state of all open work
