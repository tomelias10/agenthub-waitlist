What is an AI workflow audit?

An AI workflow audit is a focused review of repetitive business processes — lead intake, follow-up, CRM updates, scheduling, inbox triage, reporting, and customer communication — to decide which workflow should be automated first, what should stay human-reviewed, and which systems the AI employee must connect to.

What makes a workflow a strong automation candidate?

Revenue impact

Slow or inconsistent execution loses leads, renewals, appointments, or customer trust.

High priority
Clear repeat pattern

The work follows recognizable rules, stages, templates, or decision paths.

Automatable
Tool handoffs

The process touches email, CRM, calendars, forms, spreadsheets, documents, Slack, or a help desk.

Connectable
Safe boundaries

Routine actions can be automated while sensitive cases are drafted, routed, or approved by humans.

Audit areas Orynval reviews

Lead intake and response speed

Where inquiries arrive, how quickly they get answered, what qualification details are missing, and when warm leads fall through the cracks.

Follow-up and customer communication

Which conversations need reminders, status updates, proposal follow-up, document collection, or handoff to another team member.

CRM and operational records

Which fields, notes, tasks, statuses, and next-action dates are updated manually or left stale.

Human review rules

What the AI employee can do automatically, what it should only draft, and which cases must be escalated with context.
